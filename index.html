Equality Around the Globe

About the Cause

"We may have different religions, different languages, different colored skin, but we all belong to one human race."

Inequality is massive problem that has been going on for thousands of years. Although many inprovements have been made throughout the past century, it is not enough! People all over the world are treated differently for their race, religion, and gender. This website was created to educate people about how inequlaity still exists today and the different actions people are taking to help end this issue.
