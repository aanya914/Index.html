How To Help/Donate

What You Can Do!

Inequality is such an important issue that affects billions of people all over the world.

Change you Attitude

Change you mindset! Don't think of girls or skin color as different. Accept everyone and believe that everyone has the same opportunities and shoudl be treated the same.

Speak Out

If you see someone being treated differently for how they look or what they believe, speak out!

Donate!

There are several organization fighting for a change and to help stop inequality. Show your support and donate to these websites to help make a difference in someone's life

  • Black Women's Health Imperative
  • Center for Black Equity
  • Global Fund for Women
