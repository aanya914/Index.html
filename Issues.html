Equality Issues Around the Globe

Gender Pay Gap

In 2020, the average Amerian women makes 81 cents for every dollar made by a man. People should be payed fairly based on the amount and quality of work they do, not based on their gender or ethnicity.

Read more

Although a law was passed in 1963 that made paying people differently based on their gender illegal, it is still quite common today. Many people still believe that women should only cook, clean, and take care of children.

Education

Less than 40% of countries provide education for girls. More than half the girls in several countries are illiterate.

Read more

Imagine not being able to go to school? There are so many girls worldwide who are only taught how to clean and cook. Without an education, none of the girls will have the opportunity to reach their full potential.
